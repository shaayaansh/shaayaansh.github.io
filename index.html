Research Interests

My research is focused on natural language processing, with special emphasis on detecting opinionated and persuasive language in news. I have also worked on detecting propaganda and predicting how users perceive trustworthiness of different news articles.

As a component of my research on NLP problems, I've evolved strong passion for machine learning and large language models (LLMs). I have worked on unsupervised domain adaptation techniques for NLP and that is one of my research interests focused on using ML to have more robust models for different NLP tasks.
